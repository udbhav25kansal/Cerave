The Creative Concept

"The Skin Stack" Challenge

Frame CeraVe products as "the optimal skin stack"—borrowing language from supplements and fitness optimization.

The Mechanic

  • Step 1: Fitness/looksmaxing creators share their "Skin Stack" (AM + PM routine)
  • Step 2: Dermatologists "verify" the stack with science explanations
  • Step 3: Viewers share their own stacks with #SkinStack
  • Step 4: TikTok Shop integration for direct purchase
The Hook: "You optimize your supplements. You optimize your workouts. Why is your skin routine still random?"

Michael Cera Callback

Optional cameo where Michael Cera "tries to join the looksmaxing movement" but dermatologists remind him he didn't develop CeraVe.

Maintains campaign continuity while evolving the conversation.
UGC Potential:

Challenge format is inherently shareable. "What's your stack?" becomes a conversation starter.
