# CeraVe Haircare Challenge - Complete Case Brief

## THE CHALLENGE (Exact Requirements)

**Goal:** CeraVe aims to become the **global leader in dermatologist-developed scalp and haircare.**

**Deliverable:** A **one-year marketing strategy** addressing:

1. **How can CeraVe differentiate within a saturated haircare market?**
2. **How can the brand position scalp health as an essential extension of skincare?**
3. **Which innovations, partnerships, digital tools, and communication channels can help the brand earn leadership in this category?**
4. **How can CeraVe increase awareness, trial, and long-term loyalty among consumers aged 18-35?**

**Requirements:** Proposal must be focused, achievable, grounded in data, consumer insights, and CeraVe's scientific heritage.

| Metric | Value |

| 2024 Global Value | **$103.94 Billion** |
| Growth Rate (2025-2030) | **6.4%** |

### The "Skinification" Trend
> "Haircare is being fundamentally repositioned as scalp care rather than fiber care. The shift moves from cosmetic treatment of strands to biological care of the scalp ecosystem."

**Key Insight:** Consumers now treat scalp with same care as facial skin, seeking skincare ingredients in haircare products.

### Scalp vs. Face: Why It Matters
- Scalp has **2x more oil glands** than face
- Scalp has **10x more hair follicles**
- Scalp is prone to dryness, irritation, buildup
- Same skincare actives work on scalp (ceramides, niacinamide, HA)

## 5. THE FOUR STRATEGIC QUESTIONS

### Q1: How can CeraVe differentiate in a saturated haircare market?

**Key Differentiators:**
- Only brand with #1 derm-recommended skincare reputation extending to haircare
- Proprietary ceramide + niacinamide + HA formula (proven in skincare)
- "Scalp is skin" scientific positioning
- Affordable derm-quality ($9.97)
- Fragrance-free, sensitive-scalp friendly

### Q2: How can the brand position scalp health as an essential extension of skincare?

**Positioning Strategy:**
- "Your scalp is just skin with hair" messaging
- Same trusted ingredients, new category
- Routine extension (face → scalp)
- Dermatologist education content
- Before/after scalp health visuals

### Q3: Which innovations, partnerships, digital tools, and communication channels?

**Potential Innovations:**
- Scalp diagnostic tool (AI-powered)
- Personalized routine quiz
- Scalp health tracker app
- AR try-on for hair concerns

**Potential Partnerships:**
- Dermatologists/trichologists (scalp specialists)
- Hairstylists (salon channel)
- Wellness influencers (holistic health angle)
- Fitness influencers (post-workout scalp care)

**Digital Tools:**
- TikTok Shop integration
- Instagram Shopping
- Virtual consultations
- Educational content hub

**Communication Channels:**
- TikTok (primary for Gen Z)
- Instagram (Reels, Stories)
- YouTube (education)
- Reddit (community credibility)
- In-store (Walmart, CVS, Target)

### Q4: How can CeraVe increase awareness, trial, and long-term loyalty (18-35)?

**Awareness:**
- Influencer partnerships
- Paid social campaigns
- PR with derm endorsements
- #ScalpIsSkin educational content

**Trial:**
- Sampling programs
- Travel-size bundles
- "Complete the routine" promotions (skincare → haircare)
- Money-back guarantee

**Loyalty:**
- Subscription options
- Rewards program
- Community building
- Consistent results (product efficacy)
